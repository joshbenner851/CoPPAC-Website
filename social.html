Social Events

  • You are here:
  • Social
  • Home

This year, CoPPAC will be hosting a variety of social events, including group trips, company tours, and IM sports teams.

Below you can find a tentative schedule for these events and see what you have to look forward to!

Pack Expo
As a group, we will be attending Pack Expo from November 6th - 8th. This trip will be a great opportunity to network and connect with alumni, industry professionals, and your fellow packaging peers.

Company Tours
CoPPAC will be visiting a variety of companies this semester to help our members not only learn about the Packaging industry, but to give them a chance to see it firsthand.

• Amcor: September 22nd
• KTM Industries: October 5th
• Kellogg Tour: October 27th from 8:00am-12:30pm
• Mall City Containers/Newhall Klein: December 9th

Movie Outings
CoPPAC will get together every second Friday of the month this semester to head over to Wells Hall and see the free movies. Exact times and movie choices will be determined the week of the event, but keep these Fridays available on your calendar!

• October 21st
• November 11th

CoPPAC Group Dinner
Twice throughout fall semester we will be inviting all CoPPAC members to meet us in a private dining room on campus for a CoPPAC Group Dinner. These two dinners will take place on Monday, October 10 as well as Monday, November 21 from 5:00-9:00pm in the Brody Large Private Dining Room. Watch out for more details coming your way soon.

Member Study Time
Every other Thursday evening from 7:30-9:00pm in Room 50 of the MSU Union, beginning on September 22nd. CoPPAC will provide you with a quiet room to study and get your schoolwork done. Attending our Member Study Time will not only give you a place to focus, but also provides a room full of your packaging peers to seek help from, work with, and get to know.

IM Sports Teams
This fall, CoPPAC will be competing in not one, but two IM Sports Leagues. We will start the year with Sand Volleyball running from September 11th - October 6th and finish out the semester with Dodgeball, competing from October 2nd - November 3rd. Information on how to join can be found on our CoPPAC Facebook page, or contact Victoria Jansz at janszvic@msu.edu with any further questions.

Haunted Aud
On Friday October 28th, CoPPAC will be going to the MSU Auditorium for Haunted Aud. Contact Victoria Jansz at janszvic@msu.edu for more information. The event is shared on our Facebook page.

"Pop the Rock"
Thank you to everyone who helped Pop the Rock! It was a huge success.

Ag Olympics
Sign up by October 27th, event is November 10th.
