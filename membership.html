Why Join

CoPPAC isn’t just a place to network with professionals, but also with your fellow peers. Look forward to bi-weekly meetings where you get to learn more about companies and potential future employers, but also engage with your classmates and future co-workers. From study groups to IM sports teams, CoPPAC provides many outlets for students to get to know each other and have some fun outside of the classroom. With all the opportunities that CoPPAC has to offer, you’ll be sure to not only grow your professional network but also make great friends along the way.

Benefits

Your CoPPAC membership includes:


Membership in TAPPI, or the Technical Association of Pulp and Paper Industry, was founded in 1915 as a non-profit organization composed of engineers, scientists, and others in the pulp and paper industry. Today, they are focused on innovation and emerging technologies, especially in the packaging sector.

With your TAPPI membership you will receive access to:
  1. Industry Standards
  2. Networking Opportunities
  3. Conferences
  4. Scholarships
  5. An e-libary of the latest technical advancements and solutions.
  6. Leadership development

Along with all these benefits, you will also receive industry updates sent directly to your email.

Visit their website at www.tappi.org
Membership in ISTA, or the International Safe Transit Association, is an organization that focuses on transport shipping and the concerns that surround it. ISTA is the leading industry developer of testing protocols and design standards for transport packaging to ensure the integrity of contents when they arrive.

With your ISTA membership you will receive:
  1. Online access to current testing procedures and guidelines
  2. Technical expertise from the ISTA staff
  3. Eligibility to achieve CPLP (Certified Packaging Laboratory Professional) certification
  4. Access to a free copy of the ISTA Resource Book
  5. Networking opportunities

Along with all of these benefits, you will also receive subscriptions to ISTA's digital magazine and newsletter containing ISTA and industry news.

Visit their website at www.ista.org


You will also receive:
  1. Meetings with attendance and presentations from industry professionals and top companies
  2. Your resume included in a book given to multiple companies
  3. Access to study groups, IM sports teams, and many other fun social events
  4. Free food at every meeting

Cost

The cost is $75 for a full year membership. Please visit our office during office hours to pay dues.
