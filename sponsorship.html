As the School of Packaging continues to grow, CoPPAC strives to do the same. This year we are launching our Sponsorship Program to give more companies even more opportunities to connect and engage with our Spartans.

Since CoPPAC has a limited number of meetings per semester, the Sponsorship Program was developed to help industry connect and interact with students outside of our regular meetings and events. As Spartans continue to learn more about Packaging at Michigan State University, we want to make sure that companies are able to easily network and build relationships with our students and your future employees. This program allows your company to increase exposure and interaction with students, and most importantly provides the necessary platform for industry and Spartan Packaging Engineers to connect.

There are two sponsorship levels within the program that offer an increasing array of benefits at varying membership rates to suit your company’s specific networking and student outreach goals.


Go White Sponsorship Level - $225.00

Mentorship Connection
Develop relationships and interact personally with students by starting a mentorship program between your company and CoPPAC members. We will connect you with our members and help you choose the right format for your team. Whether it’s weekly Skype calls or sit down meetings once a month on campus, there are so many ways to connect with our members.

Social Media Spotlight
Make sure you’re seen by our members on all of our social media platforms and get great advertising at an affordable price! Utilizing social media is a fun and easy way to make sure you stay connected and in touch with Spartans across the school.


Go Green Sponsorship Level - $500.00

Mentorship Connection
Develop relationships and interact personally with students by starting a mentorship program between your company and CoPPAC members. We will connect you with our members and help you choose the right format for your team. Whether it’s weekly Skype calls or sit down meetings once a month on campus, there are so many ways to connect with our members.

Social Media Spotlight
Make sure you’re seen by our members on all of our social media platforms and get great advertising at an affordable price! Utilizing social media is a fun and easy way to make sure you stay connected and in touch with Spartans across the school.

Resume Book
Career fairs can be stressful for everyone involved and CoPPAC understands how important recruitment and placement is for industry and students. The resume book allows your company to see the top talent in the School of Packaging before our career fair in January. We listened to your feedback and want to make sure you are able to connect with students earlier and are excited to have this new service available to our Go Green Sponsors!

Industry “Resume Book”
This unique twist on the traditional resume book will provide our members with the opportunity to better understand what your company has to offer. From location, to descriptions of available positions, to an overview of the company, this is the perfect tool to attract top talent to your organization before the big day in January.


Please reach out to Hanna Holmi at holmihan@msu.edu to register and learn more about CoPPAC’s Sponsorship Program.
